![[100. media/image/PzIrNfiE.png|200]]
humorous way of saying that something will never happen. The phrase is used to express skepticism or disbelief regarding the occurrence of a certain event or situation.

**Synonyms:**

- Never
- Impossibly
- Highly unlikely
- Inconceivable
